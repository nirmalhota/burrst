Project Details

Collection Name: AJ1 Boom

Mint Price: 9 SOL

Supply: 11

Launch: 04/11 12pmCST

AJ1 Boom is a collection of 11 unique NFTs of Andrew Jones built on the Solana blockchain. Andrew’s project is about giving back to his nonprofit and Jimmy V Foundation to help cancer research as he progresses his career to the next level in the NBA.

 


How To Buy



  1. Buy Solana (SOL) on Coinbase or another exchange here. You will need at least 9 SOL to mint The AJ1 Boom NFT (plus a little more with exchange fees).
  2. Create a Phantom wallet on their website at phantom.app then send your SOL from the exchange you used to your Solana wallet address.
  3. Go to Andrew's page on our website and click “Connect Wallet” then select the number you want to “Mint”.
  4. Click “Mint” to own your exclusive The AJ1 Boom NFT.
  5. Once complete your The AJ1 Boom NFT will be secured in your Solana wallet.
