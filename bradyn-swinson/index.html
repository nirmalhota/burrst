Project Details

Collection Name: 44 Underdog

Mint Price: 0.6 SOL

Supply: 101

Launch: 1/28 12pmCST

44 Underdog is a collection of 101 unique art NFTs of Bradyn Swinson built on the Solana blockchain. Bradyn’s project is about bringing his fans closer together in an exclusive community where his fans will be rewarded for their long-term loyalty throughout his football career.

 


How To Buy



  1. Buy Solana (SOL) on Coinbase or another exchange here. You will need at least 0.6 SOL to mint a 44 Underdog NFT (plus a little more with exchange fees).
  2. Create a Phantom wallet on their website at phantom.app then send your SOL from the exchange you used to your Solana wallet address.
  3. Go to Bradyn’s page on our website and click “Connect Wallet” then select the number you want to “Mint”.
  4. Click “Mint” to own your exclusive 44 Underdog NFT.
  5. Once complete your 44 Underdog NFT will be secured in your Solana wallet.
